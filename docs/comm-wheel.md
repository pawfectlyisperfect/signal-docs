# Comm Wheel

The comm wheel is a quick radial menu for calling out a location without typing anything. Hold the keybind, pick a wedge, done. It places a ping waypoint at where you're looking, tinted with your squad's color.

## The eight callouts

| Callout | Icon |
|---|---|
| Rally Point | flag |
| Enemy Spotted | eye |
| Attack Here | swords |
| Defend Here | shield |
| Reinforce Here | shield-plus |
| Supplies Needed | package |
| Fall Back Here | corner-up-left |
| Area Clear | check-circle |

A single tap defaults to Rally Point, and a double tap defaults to Enemy Spotted, so the two most common callouts don't even need the wheel open.

## Where it goes

Pings go out to your own squad and any squad you're linked to, not the whole group. That keeps callouts relevant to whoever's actually coordinating together.

## Cooldown and muting

There's a short server-enforced cooldown between pings so the wheel can't be spammed. If you're muted, your pings won't go through either, same as chat.

## Lifespan

Pings aren't permanent, they last around 30 seconds and fade out smoothly as they get close to expiring, instead of just blinking and then vanishing.
